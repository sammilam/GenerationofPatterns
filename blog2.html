Pattern Blog

This blog comprises journal entries based on my experience with everyday patterns. My thesis hopes to investigate the psychological affinity and emotional effects of patterns on humans. By doing so, I use psychological strategies such as attention and emotional theories, Cannon-Bard and James Lange's.

This consistent repetition of photographing, sketching, and reflecting allowed me to discover alternative ways to look at patterns and myself. This process interests different perspectives we superimpose on designed artifacts and how creating/selecting describes us. As such, design is a cycle of repetition, like patterns.
